Salt Shaker

The salt shaker is an item which is used to salt blocks. Right click on a dirt or grass block, and it will become a Salted Dirt block. This item has 11 uses.



Salted Dirt

Salted dirt is the salted version of dirt, which is made by right-clicking a block of dirt or grass with a Salt Shaker.



Dirt

Dirt is the a base block in Minecraft, which can be used for growing crops or trees. It can be made by combining Salted Dirtand a bucket of water.
