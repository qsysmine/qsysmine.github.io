Bell Schedule

Why?

Last year, the administration at Dublin High School released a new, confusing bell schedule. The school often has variations on this schedule which trip up students.

What?

The DHS Bell Schedule App is an iOS app and widget which make the bell schedule simple. A large piece of text shows you when your class ends, and the rest of the schedule is at your fingertips.

How?

This app was made in XCode using Swift (and including the library RSBarcodes).
