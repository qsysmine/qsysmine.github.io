Bell Schedule

Why?

There was a new bell schedule at my school and the students were having trouble remembering it.

What?

To fix this, I made an app and widget that used the iPhone's NSDate API to tell you when your current class was about to end.

How?

I wrote all the code for the app in XCode using Swift. This was, in addition, a good way to learn & practise Apple's new language.

So?

To get my app, click here to download it from the App Store.
