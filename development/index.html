Bell Schedule

Why?

In 2016, I entered Dublin High, a school with a new, confusing bell schedule. The nature of the schedule often trips up students who just want to get to their next class on time.

What?

The DHS Bell Schedule App is an iOS and Android app which make the bell schedule simple. The glanceable design allows students to quickly know how much time they have left.

Iron Horse Mafia Distance App

Why?

The Dublin High School cross country team, otherwise known as the Iron Horse Mafia, is one of the best cross country teams in the state of California. Despite this aptitude, we had trouble focussing during our core workouts.

What?

The Iron Horse Mafia Distance App is an iOS and Android app which times the core circuits for the team. It contains a list of all of the circuits and all of the exercises within them. When the team does a circuit, it lets the leader see what's coming up next and gives audible feedback when an exercise is over.
